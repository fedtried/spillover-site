Frequently asked questions

Can I still access blocked apps in an emergency?

In soft mode, yes — tap "open for 10 mins" for temporary access. In hard mode, no — that's the point. Choose your mode carefully.

What if I capture a thought but want to add more?

The capture screen stays open after each save. Keep typing, keep tapping done.

Does it work if I restart my phone?

Yes. Blocking resumes automatically based on your schedule.

Can I block Safari or system apps?

Some system apps can't be blocked due to iOS limitations. Most third-party apps and Safari work fine.

What happens to my thoughts if I delete the app?

They're deleted with the app. Export anything important first using copy or the Reminders integration.

Does this use a lot of battery?

No. The app uses Apple's native scheduling APIs, which are highly optimized.

Why do I need to enable Screen Time?

Spillover uses Apple's Screen Time framework to block apps. This is the only way to reliably prevent app access on iOS. Your Screen Time data stays on your device.

The block screen isn't appearing

Make sure Screen Time is enabled in your device Settings, and that you've granted Spillover permission to manage Screen Time. Try restarting the app after granting permissions.

I'm not getting morning notifications

Check that notifications are enabled for Spillover in Settings > Notifications. Also verify your morning reminder time is set correctly in the app.

Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone (optimized for all sizes)
  • Screen Time must be enabled
