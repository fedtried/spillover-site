Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version

Your thoughts stay on your device. We don't collect them, we don't see them, we don't sell them.

Data we collect

None.

Spillover does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your captured thoughts are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leave your phone.

No account required

Spillover does not require you to create an account. There's no sign-up, no email collection, no user profiles.

No cloud sync

Your data is not synced to any cloud service. It exists only on your device. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

No analytics

We do not use analytics tools to track how you use the app. We don't know how often you open it, what you type, or when you use it.

Permissions we request

  • Screen Time — Required to block apps during quiet hours. This uses Apple's Family Controls framework.
  • Notifications — Optional. Used to remind you to review your captured thoughts in the morning.
  • Reminders — Optional. Allows you to add captured thoughts directly to Apple Reminders.

These permissions are processed entirely on-device by Apple's frameworks. No data is sent to us.

Third-party services

Spillover does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Data deletion

Delete the app to delete all your data. You can also delete individual thoughts from within the app at any time.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. But our commitment is simple: your data stays yours.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@spillover.app
